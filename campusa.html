The Campus Ambassador Program

Hello friends,

We are glad to invite you to be a part of the first ever TEDx event of Medi-Caps University TEDxMediCapsUniversity 2019 an independently organised TEDx event, to be held on 18th February, 2019 at the Medi-Caps University by being one of our Campus Ambassadors.

TEDxMediCapsUniversity 2019: Changing Era, focuses on bringing together some great minds and influencers who are actively contributing to draw on a change in the society, to foster learning, inspiration and provoke conversations that matter, you are invited to join us on 18th February 2019, and take part in the event. We hope to reignite the fire of curiosity, which separates a child from an adult, and enrich the thinking of all people visiting us.

What we're hoping from you, as a Campus Ambassador, would be to be a representative for TEDxMediCapsUniversity 2019 in your college. You, being an integrated member of TEDxMediCapsUniversity 2019, would be provided a certificate from TEDxMediCapsUniversity 2019, Medi-Caps University along with other perks and incentives including official TEDxMediCapsUniversity 2019 merchandise, a free pass for TEDxMediCapsUniversity 2019 and much more.


This is what you're looking at if you help make TEDxMediCapsUniversity 2019 a grand success:

  •   Certificate from TEDxMediCapsUniversity 2019

  •   Official TEDxMediCapsUniversity 2019 Merchandise

  •   Pass to the event

  •   Opportunity to meet TEDxMediCapsUniversity 2019 headliners

  •   Other exciting prizes

Note: Last date for registration is 18 January 2019

Terms and Conditions:

  •   Perks mentioned are awarded based on a point based system and points are rewarded when CAs successfully perform various tasks and provides certain evidence of the same.

  •   Points will be rewarded when CAs like and follow TEDxMediCapsUniversity FaceBook and Instagram page and share posts and stick posters on college public notice boards.

  •   Pass to the event will be provided based on the number of registrations done with your unique code.

  •   Merchandise of the event will be provided based on the number of registrations done with your unique code.

  •   Certificates will be mailed to CAs after the event.

  •   Changes in the reward scheme before or after the selection of Campus Ambassadors is under the sole discretion of TEDxMediCapsUniversity, however changes would be notified to the CAs as soon as possible.
