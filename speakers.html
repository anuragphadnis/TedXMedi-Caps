HG Gauranga Das Prabhu


Director, Govardhan Ecovillage

An intriguing and a proficient leader, Gauranga Das is a graduate from IIT Bombay, currently serving in various capacities at national and global level in ISKCON organization. He is the director of Govardhan Ecovillage,a United Nations World Tourism Organization (UNWTO) award winning Eco-village community. He inspires people to attain spirituality by presenting his knowledge on Vedas and Bhagavad Gita in a contemporary and enticing way. He has represented ISKCON as a speaker in various conferences and has given more than 10,000 discourses on Srimad Bhagvatam, Bhagavad Gita amongst other scriptures.

Parikipandla Narahari


IAS Officer

P Narahari is an Indian Civil Servant, employed by the Indian Administrative Services , who has authored two books namely "Who owns Mhow?" and "The Making of Ladli Laxmi Yojna". He is one of India's few civil servants who interacts with the citizens of India through social media platforms to solve their problems. As an ace District Collector, he played a major role in making Gwalior, a barrier-free city and Indore, the cleanest city of India, thus setting an example for other cities of the country. Mr. Narahari has been praised with almost 40 awards for his excellent performance in his administrative career till now.

Sagar Dodeja


Ex IES Officer

The man amidst the sea of information who strives to be a beacon of light for the budding engineers and aspirants all around India, Sagar Dodeja is one of the leading educational vlogger, teacher and motivator of our nation. He is an IES officer and is a living example of the triumph of hard work and grit over luck in this changing era of education and industrial sector. He has over 1.2 million subscribers on YouTube and quite known for his outspokenness and genuine advices to students

Capt. Shalini Singh


Mrs. India Earth, Mrs.India Classic 2017

Captain Shalini Singh is one of the modern icons of women empowerment, both as a sword and a shield to our nation. Her husband is a posthumous recipient of Kirti Chakra and she walked into his footsteps into the battlefield as a Captain in Indian Army. She has also won Mrs India Earth & Mrs. India Classic 2017. She is a public figure, motivational speaker and has already given TED talk in TEDx IMI, New Delhi. A true multidimensional personality and a role model to all women of our society.

Pooja Gor


Actress

A talented and popular actress and a fresh entrant to the Indian film industry. Pooja Gor is a multifaceted personality with a stupedous history of 10 years in television with 13 full length soap operas, 2 reality shows and 1 feature film "Kedarnath". A woman of talent, grit and a box full of experience and insights to share.

Durga Gawde



Sculptor, Educator, Activist and India's first performing drag king.Durga is a prismatic personality and one of the vanguards of spreading awareness about gender fluidity and non binary identities. Durga is an RISD alumnus and a visiting faculty in ISDI. PARSON's Mumbai and a fellow teacher at urbanframe, MIT, USA. Durga is a person with insights about art. Technology, design and identity. A unique disposition combined with a global exposure, Durga is a speaker to be lent one's ears too.

Kapil Jadhwani



Kapil is a food enthusisat & one of the Indore's most loved social media influencers. His instagram page - Indore Food Explorer has more than 110,000 followers and is growing everyday. He is pioneer of food photography in Indore. Started his venture while in first year of college. His journey of analyzing the public demand and creating his own market is truly inspirational.
